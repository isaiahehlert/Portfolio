## 🎓 Certification Description
The CompTIA A+ certification validates foundational IT skills required for troubleshooting, supporting, and maintaining modern computer systems, operating systems, and networks. It covers both hardware and software technologies, as well as security, networking, and operational procedures used in real-world IT environments.

---

## ✅ Skills Gained
- Hardware and software troubleshooting  
- Operating system support (Windows, macOS, Linux basics)  
- IT security fundamentals and best practices  
- Networking concepts and device connectivity  
- System maintenance and operational procedures
